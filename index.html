Not sure how to play?

Objective:

The goal of Rock ✊🏼, Paper 🖐🏼, Scissors ✌🏼 is to choose the move that defeats your opponent's move.

The Rules:

  1. Two players: You and an opponent play against each other.
  2. The choices: Each player chooses one of three items: Rock ✊🏼, Paper 🖐🏼, or Scissors ✌🏼.

How to Win

  • Rock✊🏼beats Scissors✌🏼
  • Scissors✌🏼beats Paper🖐🏼
  • Paper🖐🏼beats Rock✊🏼

After the reveal, the winner is decided based on the rules above. If you win, you score a point!

Ties

If both players choose the same gesture (e.g., both choose Rock), it's a tie.
